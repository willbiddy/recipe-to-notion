Recipe Clipper for Notion

Save recipes from any website to your Notion database with one click. No more copying and pasting—just paste a recipe URL and get a beautifully formatted Notion page with cover photos, organized ingredients, and AI-powered tags.

How it works

1

Paste a recipe URL from any website—we support most recipe sites including NYT Cooking, AllRecipes, and more.

2

We extract the recipe data using structured data (JSON-LD) and intelligent HTML parsing to capture ingredients, instructions, and images.

3

Claude AI analyzes the recipe to automatically add cuisine tags, meal types (breakfast, lunch, dinner), healthiness scores (0-10), and ingredient categories.

4

A beautiful Notion page is created with cover photos, organized ingredients grouped by shopping aisle, numbered instructions, and all the AI-generated metadata for easy searching and filtering.

Setup required

Before using this tool, you'll need to deploy it to Vercel and configure your Notion database. See setup instructions for detailed steps.

Paste a recipe URL from any website. The recipe will be scraped, analyzed, and saved to your Notion database.

Settings

Get this from your Vercel deployment's API_SECRET environment variable. This is stored locally in your browser and never shared.
